Text to order or text to buy has been tipped as the next big thing in eCommerce.
Because retailers generally get most of their traffic from mobile devices,
marketers stand to gain a lot from adopting a strategy
that makes buying from mobile completely hitch-free.

SMS contests are a great way to drive customer engagement.
Keep in mind, however, that some customers may not be keen on participating
in contests making it even more important to pay attention to opt-out best practices.
Make it easy for subscribers to opt out or unsubscribe.
